About Trainer Riad Thalji

PMP Trainer Riad Thalji is one of the best project management and IT trainers in the Middle East and North Africa.  He provides his training in many countries and has excellent experience in this field.  He is one of the trainers who come from the real-life business.

Riad Thalji worked as IT Analysis Department Manager at Orange Telecom, operated by France Telecom in Jordan.  His name appeared on PMI PMBOK 5th Edition as one of the reviewers.

Trainer Riad Thalji main goal is to spend his life in human resource development through providing professional courses that target competitive job opportunities.

He provided his courses to experts in governments, construction firms, IT in Arab Countries, France and Paris.

His book "A Practical Approach to PMP Exam Preparation" was listed in amazon.com, and provided another approach for PMP Preparation.
